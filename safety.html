Safety

Ethanol

Before each procedure, we sterilized our lab area using high-concentration ethanol. The ethanol kills a majority of the bacteria and viruses in the environment. Working in a sterile environment helps us achieve proper results without any outside forces to potentially disrupt our processes.

Pipette Tips

After each use, we replaced the tip of all the pipettes to avoid residue continuing into later stages of our experiment and future ones. This was another measure to maintain utmost sterility in our working environment.

Surgical Gloves

Throughout each experiment, we use surgical gloves to avoid bringing in extranneous material from our environment and taking any from the experiment outside of it.
