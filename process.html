Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)

PCR is a technique used to replicate small sections of DNA. We used this process both in the 3A assembly kit and in our project. It was successful in our 3A assembly as our ecoli cells changed colors but in our project our DNA did not replicate properly and we think this was because the strand was so long.

Gel Electrophoresis

Gel electrophoresis is a technique used to determine if certain strands of DNA where made. We used this technique when trying to PCR the DNA sequence for bornyl diphosphate synthetase.

Centrifuge

We used the centrifuge at several stages throughout our project. It helps separate different contents of a test tube by weight. It was also used occasionally to bring all of the liquids in a test tube to the bottom of the test tube for easy pipetting.
