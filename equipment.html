Equipment

Chemical Shower

Our lab area was permanently equipped with a chemical shower and eye washer in case our chemicals were to get a bit out of hand.

Ethanol

Before each procedure, we sterilized our lab area using high-concentration ethanol. The ethanol kills a majority of the bacteria and viruses in the environment.

Fire Blanket

Our lab area was permanently equipped with a fire blanket in case our extinguisher was unable to take care of any possible fires.

Fire Extinguisher

Our lab area was permanently equipped with an industrial-grade fire extinguisher so that if the need was to rise, we would be able adequately to respond.

Gel Electrophoresis

We used this to separate and analyze macromolecules based on physical characteristics.

Pipettes

Pipettes enabled us to accurately and precisely measure liquids throughout our project. After every use, we replaced the disposable tips.

Surgical Gloves

Throughout each experiment, we use surgical gloves to avoid bringing in extraneous material from our environment.

Thermal Cycler

We used a thermal cycler, or PCR machine, to enlarge segments of DNA during the PCR process.
